Theia

Your personal chief of staff, in your pocket.

Theia is an AI-powered coach for iPhone that helps you run your life like someone has your back — because now something does. Set goals, build habits, protect your focus with Screen Time limits, and talk through your day with a coach that actually knows your context: your priorities, your calendar, your streaks, and (only if you choose) your health trends.

Everything you tell Theia stays yours. Your data lives on your device and in your private iCloud — we run no accounts and keep no copy of your conversations. Read exactly how it works in our privacy policy.

Support & FAQ

How do I get help or report a bug?

Email us at gabrielwang.dev@gmail.com. Include your iOS version and a description of what happened — we read every message.

How is my data handled?

Your goals, habits, and chats are stored on your device and synced through your private iCloud. Coaching messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude via our secure backend and are not retained on our servers. Full details in the privacy policy.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all data from your device. To also remove synced data, delete Theia's iCloud data in Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Account Storage. Health, calendar, and Screen Time access can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings.

Why does Theia ask for Health, Calendar, or Screen Time access?

All integrations are optional. Health and calendar data give your coach real context for better guidance; Screen Time access lets Theia shield the apps you ask it to. You choose exactly what to share, and you can change your mind anytime.

Can I request a feature?

Absolutely — email gabrielwang.dev@gmail.com. Theia is actively developed and shaped by what its users need.
