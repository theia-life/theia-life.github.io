Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Email gabrielwang.dev@gmail.com with your iOS version and a description of what happened. Screenshots are always helpful.

How is my data handled?

Your goals, habits, and conversations are stored on your device and synced through your own private iCloud. Coaching messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude via our secure backend and are not retained on our servers. See the privacy policy for full details.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all local data. To clear synced iCloud data, go to Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage and remove Theia. Health, calendar, and Screen Time permissions can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings.

Why does Theia need Health, Calendar, or Screen Time access?

All integrations are optional. Health and calendar data give your coach real context for better guidance; Screen Time access lets Theia enforce focus blocks you set up. You control exactly what to share and can change your mind at any time.

The coach isn't responding — what should I do?

Make sure you have an active internet connection. If the issue persists, force-quit and reopen the app. Still stuck? Email us and we'll sort it out.

Can I request a feature?

Yes — email gabrielwang.dev@gmail.com. Theia is actively developed and shaped by what its users need.
